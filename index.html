Exploring Correlation Between Poverty and Veteran Status

The variable for veterans represents the percentage of participants who answered "yes" to the census question "Have you ever served on active duty in the United States Armed Forces?" The poverty variable represents the percentage of participants who fall beneath the poverty level as determined by the Census Bureau's metrics.

The correlation coefficient was .065 meaning there was no linear relationship.
